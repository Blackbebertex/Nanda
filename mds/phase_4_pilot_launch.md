# Phase 4 — Pilot Launch

**Duration:** 8–12 weeks

## Pilot Scope
- Limited customer group
- Limited product categories
- Human review for recommendations
- Controlled RM group
- Strict monitoring
- No autonomous execution

## Activities
1. Enable pilot cohort.
2. Monitor adoption.
3. Review recommendation quality.
4. Track escalations.
5. Review complaints.
6. Monitor hallucination and safety metrics.
7. Collect user feedback.
8. Tune rules and prompts.
9. Conduct weekly compliance review.
10. Decide production-readiness.

## Deliverables
- Pilot launch report
- Weekly performance reports
- Customer feedback summary
- RM feedback summary
- Compliance review log
- Updated backlog
- Production-readiness assessment
